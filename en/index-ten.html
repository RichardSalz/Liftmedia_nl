About this business

LiftMedia Nederland is the pioneer of elevator advertising in the Dutch market, offering a unique 'captive audience' marketing solution that leverages waiting moments to deliver highly effective, measurable results. With a proven European track record, our expert team provides local businesses with an innovative, cost-effective way to boost sales and brand awareness by reaching residential communities directly in enclosed elevator spaces. Join us to be a first mover in shaping the future of elevator advertising in the Netherlands.

Captive audience marketing

LiftMedia Nederland is the pioneer of 'captive audience' marketing and the Dutch adaptation of successful elevator advertising in other EU countries! Our two-person expert team – an experienced CEO and an Operations director – has already proven in Europe how effective elevator advertising can be as a marketing tool, where research shows that every EUR spent on out-of-home advertising generates 5.97 EUR in product sales. No one else in the Dutch market offers this service, which provides a unique position for us and our clients. Our approach is simple and practical: we leverage those moments when people have nothing else to do but wait. Research indicates that elevator advertising can result in 1.5 times sales increase and 1.4 times ROI improvement.

The psychological impact of elevator advertising is particularly strong: it provides an escape route from awkward silence situations, thus triggering positive emotions. We offer direct, fact-based solutions to our Dutch business partners that deliver measurable results, and in accordance with the practical Dutch business culture, we formulate a clear value proposition. Our service is particularly effective for local businesses who want to promote their shops or services operating near residential buildings. As advertisements placed in the enclosed space of elevators provide a minimum of 60 exposures per month per resident.

In the Netherlands, this is a completely new marketing channel, which provides significant competitive advantage for our clients as first movers.

Our service is ideal for local businesses, service providers, and any companies that wants to directly reach residential communities with a cost-effective, innovative marketing solution that complements digital strategies. Join our Dutch clients and be one of them to apply this innovative marketing solution in the Netherlands! Together, let's write a Dutch success story of elevator advertising and shape the Dutch standards for elevator advertising!
