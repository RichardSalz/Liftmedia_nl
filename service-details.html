For Advertisers:

The service consists of placing attractive, printed advertising material on the lifts of office and residential buildings, on the interior side walls. These advertisements are displayed in physical form rather than on digital displays, making them more cost-effective, more personal and more distinctive from digital advertising noise.

Why is it worth signing a contract with us?

Direct, focused reach

Guaranteed attention: An elevator is an enclosed space where there are few distractions for passengers. This means that advertisements reach almost everyone who uses the building.

Multiple daily encounters:

Residents and workers encounter your message several times a day, which increases brand recall and the chance for action.

Office buildings

An office elevator makes an average of 9,000-16,500 trips per month. This provides exceptionally high exposure for advertisements, especially among the affluent business audience. The post-COVID-19 return has stabilized at 60-72%, which still represents significant daily traffic.

Residential buildings

The residential building segment is particularly attractive because residents use elevators regularly and repeatedly. Such premium locations offer an excellent opportunity for high-value advertising campaigns.

Efficiency arguments

According to surveys, 79-84% of passengers view advertisements placed in elevators, and 34% have purchased or used a service advertised in this way. This rate is outstanding compared to traditional offline advertising tools, so we offer a proven effective platform for advertisers.

There are few distractions during the time spent in the elevator, so attention is focused. Suitable for both local and national campaigns, easily segmentable. QR codes, coupons, and exclusive offers make the campaign measurable and interactive. Local and national targeting

Appears exactly where your target audience lives or works

Select a city, district, or building type so that your ad will appear in the most relevant locations.

Particularly effective for local businesses, service providers, events, and national brands.

Simple Steps to Process

Selecting target groups and locations

You decide in which cities, districts, and types of buildings you would like to place your advertisements.

Submitting or designing creative material

You can provide your own graphics or request a complete design.

Launching and placing the campaign

We place the advertisements and document them with photos.

F.A.Q.

  • When we first make contact (which can be by email, phone, or in person), we discuss the location where you want to reach your target audience.
  • We discuss how much advertising space you need and how long you want your campaign to run.
  • You choose the poster format in which you would like your advertisements to appear.
  • You send us the advertising material in print-ready format. (If you do not have one, we can prepare it for you, for which we will provide a separate quote. )
  • We place the advertisement and take a photo of it, which we send to you.

  • If your advertising material is already in print-ready format, then 10 (ten) working days before the advertisement is to be placed.
  • If you entrust us with the compilation and editing of the advertising material, then we need to start working together 15 (fifteen) days before the desired publication date.
