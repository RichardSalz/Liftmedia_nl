Business Solution for Lifts

Our company provides professional placement of printed marketing materials in the elevators of apartment buildings and office buildings in the Netherlands.

For residential buildings and office buildings:

The advertisements appear in the form of aesthetic self-adhesive posters, so the interior of the elevator always remains tidy and sophisticated. We provide a comprehensive service: we take care of every step of the process, from graphic design and printing to regular replacement and maintenance.

Why is it worthwhile for apartment buildings and office buildings to sign a contract with us?

Additional source of income

We pay a regular, fixed fee to the condominium or management company for each advertisement placed in the elevator, which can be used to reduce common costs, for maintenance, or for community purposes.

Organized, aesthetic appearance

The method we use ensures that there are no untidy advertisements in the elevators, and the appearance is always clean, sophisticated, and uniform.

Minimal administration and burden

We take care of the entire process—placement, replacement, maintenance—the operator only has to approve the contract.

Protection of residents' interests

Only advertisements with content that has been checked in advance and is acceptable to the community may be displayed: we do not place political, offensive or misleading advertisements.

Community information option

If required, the building's own messages (e.g. maintenance, general meeting) can also be displayed.

Particularly effective for local businesses, service providers, events, and national brands.

Legal and safety compliance

The posted materials do not interfere with the operation of the elevator and comply with Dutch safety and fire regulations.

Flexible, terminable contract

If the community decides it does not want the service, the contract can be terminated at any time without justification, within the legal framework specified in the contract, of course.

Quick response, guarantee

In the event of vandalism or damage, we undertake to repair it immediately, so the elevators always remain tidy.
These benefits help the condominium manager to generate additional income, while keeping the elevators in a civilized, tidy condition and protecting the interests of the residents.

F.A.Q.

  • When the homeowners' association / office building decides to sell advertising space on the interior surfaces of the elevator in their building, they contact us.
  • We discuss together what types of advertisements we can provide and which formats are best suited to the interior of the elevator.
  • We then draw up a contract specifying the size of the space to be used, the method of installation, and the payment terms.
  • After that, the condominium or office building does not need to do anything; we will contact potential customers who would like to advertise in the building.
  • We will have the labels produced and install them in the elevator.

Please notify us, and we will come and replace the damaged surface. The replacement will be at our expense, and you will not have to pay anyone anything extra.

The contract is concluded with a 30-day cancellation period. You must understand that we also have commitments to advertisers and need to know in advance when to look for other apartment buildings to purchase advertising space in a given area.

Of course, this is your decision, but we will always make suggestions based on the advertising space and quantities available to us.

The contracts are for a specific period of time and it is up to you to decide whether to extend them. However, we always recommend signing a contract for at least 3 months to allow for scalability and to see concrete results to make working with us worthwhile for you.
